The Complete Warrior Science

Origin

Born in Kerala over 3,000 years ago, Kalaripayattu is considered the oldest martial art still in existence. The word "Kalari" refers to the training ground, traditionally a sunken pit resembling a sacred temple space.

Techniques

This comprehensive system includes:

  • Meipayattu: Body conditioning and animal posture sequences
  • AngaThari: Weapons training (sword, shield, staff, urumi)
  • VerumKai: Unarmed combat techniques
  • MarmaChikitsa: Knowledge of 107 vital points

Healing Arts

Integral to Kalaripayattu is Uzhichil (traditional massage) and herbal medicine, making it a complete system of warrior rehabilitation and longevity.

Global Influence

Kalaripayattu has influenced martial arts across Asia and forms the foundation for Kerala's performing arts like Kathakali and Theyyam.
